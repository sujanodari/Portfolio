About

I set the direction,
then build it.

Technical lead and architect — with the hands to ship.

For more than eleven years I have led engineering teams and owned the architecture of the systems they build. I make the structural decisions that are expensive to reverse — how services divide, where data lives, which trade-offs are worth their cost — and I make them early, when they still matter. Based in Kathmandu, I have grown from a freelancer into a lead who can both set technical direction and remain answerable for the code itself.

What separates the work is that the architecture and the execution come from the same person. I am comfortable at every layer — interface, services, data, and infrastructure — so the design I draw on the whiteboard is one I can also build, review, and defend. At Dhuni Software I lead delivery for clients in the United States, building FDA-compliant product-lifecycle platforms that exceed commercial tools in depth and flexibility, and I have contracted directly for a Fortune 500 biopharmaceutical company.

You can hand me an ambiguous brief and a team, and get back a coherent system and a clear plan. A technical lead who keeps delivery on course, an architect who designs for the years ahead, and a full-stack engineer who can build any part of it.
